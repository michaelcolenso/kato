Construction?

Before I learned to code and transitioned into professional web development, I was a construction project engineer/project manager with substantial experience working with high performance teams on major commercial, institutional, and residential construction projects.

Building software is not entirely dissimilar to building parking structures or apartment buildings.

I think the essence of both software and construction engineering is integrating, inventing, organizing, and manipulating complex systems of (often) incompatible components to serve a unified purpose. The plethora of shared metaphors between the two industries is no accident, and I consider myself fortunate to have acquired somewhat unique perspective in both domains.
